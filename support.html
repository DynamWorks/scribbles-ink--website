Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create my first font?

Tap "New Font" on the home screen, name your project, and start tracing characters. In the free tier you can draw each character individually. Once you've traced enough characters (at least 3), go to the Build step to generate your font and try it in the Playground.

What trace modes are available?

Scribbles offers three ways to input your handwriting:

  • Individual (Free) — draw each character in its own canvas cell
  • Words (Plus) — write full words and Scribbles segments them into individual characters
  • Freeform (Pro) — write naturally on a blank canvas with automatic AI-powered character recognition

What can I do with the free tier?

Create unlimited font projects, trace all 62 characters (A-Z, a-z, 0-9), build fonts in all four weights (Light, Regular, Bold, Heavy), and test your font in the Playground where you can create and share cards with your handwriting font. To export font files or install them on your device, you'll need a Plus or Pro subscription.

How do I install my font on my device?

After building your font (Plus or Pro), tap "Install Font" in the Build step. The font will be registered on your device and available in the custom Scribbles keyboard.

How does the custom keyboard work?

Go to Settings > General > Keyboard > Keyboards > Add New Keyboard and select "Scribbles". The keyboard loads your built fonts from a shared container on your device. It does not require Full Access and does not send any data over the network.

Can I export my font to use on a computer?

Yes (Plus or Pro). Scribbles can export your font as a .ttf (TrueType) file. Pro subscribers can also export multi-weight TrueType Collection (.ttc) files and SVG glyphs. Use the share button in the Build step to export.

Can I import SVG glyphs?

Yes (Pro). If you've designed characters in another tool, you can import SVG files for individual glyphs.

What's the difference between Plus and Pro?

  • Free — unlimited projects, all characters, individual trace mode, build fonts, Playground with shareable cards
  • Plus — everything in Free, plus words trace mode, font file export, and on-device font installation
  • Pro — everything in Plus, plus freeform AI trace mode, variable font collections, SVG import/export, glyph point and metrics editing, and cursive letter connections

How do I restore my purchases?

Your subscription is tied to your Apple ID and restores automatically. If it doesn't appear, make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used to purchase, then relaunch the app.

Is my data private?

Yes. All handwriting processing happens entirely on your device. No data is sent to any server. If you use iCloud, your projects sync through your private iCloud container. See our Privacy Policy for details.
