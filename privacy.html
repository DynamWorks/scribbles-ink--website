Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 4, 2025

Overview

Scribbles.ink ("the App") is developed by DynamWorks. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains what data the App collects and how it is used.

Data Collection

Scribbles.ink does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data with DynamWorks or any third parties. All data processing happens entirely on your device.

What Stays on Your Device

  • Handwriting data — stroke drawings, glyph outlines, and font projects are stored locally using SwiftData.
  • Font files — generated TrueType fonts are stored on-device and in your App Group container (for the keyboard extension).
  • Subscription status — managed entirely by Apple's StoreKit framework. DynamWorks does not have access to your payment information.

Keyboard Extension

The Scribbles custom keyboard extension does not request or use Full Access. It cannot access the network, and it does not transmit any keystrokes or data. It reads font files from a shared App Group container on your device — nothing else.

On-Device Processing

Character recognition and segmentation uses Apple's Vision framework, which runs entirely on-device. No handwriting images or text are sent to any server.

Analytics & Tracking

Scribbles.ink does not include any analytics SDKs, crash reporting tools, or advertising frameworks. We do not track you in any way.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services other than Apple's own frameworks (StoreKit, Vision). No data is shared with third parties.

Children's Privacy

Scribbles.ink does not knowingly collect any data from children. Since no personal data is collected from any user, the App is safe for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
